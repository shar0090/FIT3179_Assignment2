Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympics


This interactive visualisation offers a overview of the data from the Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympic Games, held in 2021 due to the COVID-19 Global Pandemic, with a focus on the Swimming Event. Explore the data to see how well the Australian team performed in this event as well as overall in the games.
